How to become a Good programmer

23 November, 2022




Have you ever wondered how to write codes flawlessly without getting any error ?

Well let me tell you even the most experienced coders get errors in their day to day tasks. Coding is something in which no matter how good you become you will always face errors . So you cannot avoid errors but you can become a good coder who solves error and create solutions for that you have to learn problem solving skills , logical thinking and most important loads of practice everyday.

Some habits which are very essential to become a good coder are having patience , consistency , practicing . I call it CPP (Consistent practice with patience) these traits will help you in any walk of life. The key to master any skill or talent comes when you apply CPP in your life .

Ok so now we know that we need CPP now we will get into technical aspect where to apply CPP. To become a good coder there are few steps :-
1) Choose a programming language
2) Learn language syntax and concepts
3) Solve questions or build projects for practicing

Best way is to create projects . The more projects you will make the more you will become good at coding . And at last coding is not something you can learn in one or two days It is something which requires lots of time .

Its better to solve one problem a day but making sure you solve daily rather than solving 10 problems a day and not doing anything for a month. So this the key follow it and you are on your way to become a good coder.
